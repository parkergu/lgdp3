Real-world Experiments

To evaluate the performance of 3DVA (ours) in the real world, we conducted experiments on 3 tasks: Stack, Pour, and Hang.

Stack: The gripper grabs a block, and puts it on another block.

Pour: The gripper grabs the teapot handle, moves to the teacup, and pours the water.

Hang: The gripper grabs the mug handle, and hangs it on the mug rack.

All videos are at 1x speed.
